CEHv9 - Course Details

WHAT IS CEH?

CEHv9 is a comprehensive ethical hacking and information systems security auditing program focusing on latest security threats, advanced attack vectors and practical real time demonstration of latest hacking techniques, methodologies, tools, tricks and security measures. A program developed by subject matter experts from all over the world and are constantly updated to ensure that the students are exposed to the latest advances in the space.

WHY CEH?

The purpose of CEH is to assess the security of a network or system's infrastructure. It is a qualification obtained by assessing the security of computer systems, using penetration testing techniques. Vulnerabilities tend to be found in deprived or inadequate hardware & software configuration or all so known flaws, and weak links.

Best training for CEH?

The training dives deep into the techniques used by best hackers for achieving his goal by various modes. This also enlighten you to use the same hacking techniques to perform an ethical activity, by end of this course will uphold the knowledge and ability to efficiently assess and measure the threats to your organizational assets.
