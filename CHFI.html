C|HFI - Computer Hacking Forensics Investigator

WHAT IS CHFI V8?

As Eccouncil states, Computer hacking forensic investigation is the process of detecting hacking attacks and properly extracting evidence to report the crime and conduct audits to prevent future attacks.

A CHFI certified security professionals possess the skills and knowledge require to uncover and analyse complex digital evidence, computer Investigation techniques are being used by police, government and corporate entities globally and many of them turn to EC-Council for our Computer Hacking Forensic Investigator CHFI Certification Program.

WHY CHFI V8?

Computer crime in today’s cyber world is on the rise, it is more prevalent than ever, and the attacks are getting highly complex. More tools are invented daily for conducting Computer Investigations but it is next to impossible to identity and isolate it. The CHFI curriculum is vendor-neutral. The idea of digital forensics investigation is what computer forensics all about.

Best training for CHFI V8?

Our Eccouncil CI teaches you how to become that CHFI professional. You’ll learn how to; determine potential online criminal activity at its inception, legally gather evidence, search and investigate wireless attacks. Most importantly we ensure that you acquire an aesthetic skill set thus ensuring that the investigation and its findings are legally admissible. The certification holders can work across a wide range of computing environments.
